Photoshop

Photoshop is an Adobe product that is used to edit and adjust images. Photoshop has a variety of tools that allow you to select, delete, and adjust elements in your photos.

Some of Photoshops tools include the magic eraser, crop, clone stamp, spot healing brush, and gradient tools.


Illustrator

Illustrator is an Adobe product that is used to design and build digital graphics. Illustrator has a variety of tools that allow you to create shapes, modify text, and design icons.

Some of Illustrators tools include the pen, selection, shape, type, gradient, brush, and slice tools.

Uses of Photoshop

Photoshop is used to edit and adjust images. Some features include

  • Magic Eraser
  • Crop and Resize
  • Color Adjustment

Uses of Illustrator

Illustrator is used to build and design graphics. Some features include

  • Building/Editing Shapes
  • Typography
  • Brushes
