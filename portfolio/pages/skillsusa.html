Project Description

I built several different website pages for the skills USA competition. We were given a topic and were tasked with coding our own website with a partner in 4 hours or less. The first two screenshots were of the practice competitions I did beforehand. These practice competitions helped me improve my coding speed and helped me get an idea for what I wanted to build for the actual competition.
