Training

Different types of training will be needed for the different adventures, but here is a list of the general types of training you will need to do before you set off.

Physical Fitness

Space is a physically demanding environment, you will need to have training before your flight to prepare for any unexpected challenges. We will have you practice and get fit for the flight.

Zero Gravity

While in space gravity will be nonexistent. This can have devastating effects if you are not well prepared for it. That is why we will have you prepare in our state-of-the-art facilities. They can create microgravity-like environments so you are prepared for the change in gravity.

Training Continued,

Systems

Part of your training will include learning the systems of the ship.You will learn what does what and how to run any part of the ship you might need to. You will not learn how to fly the ship or use any of the main controls, those will be left to our experts.

Emergencies

In case of any emergencies while in space, you will learn the emergency procedures. You will learn how to act and what to do for any emergency that arrives and even go through evacuation drills.

Safety

There are many risks to space travel, but we guarantee your safety and have many precautions in place to keep you safe and have a successful and fun trip.

Medical Test

For your space adventure you will take medical tests to make sure there are no problems and you are ready for your flight.

Procedures

You will learn the procedures of the ship. What to do in emergencies, how each day will go, how to carry out actions, and who to ask if you have a question.

Safety Continued,

Training

Our training will provide you with all of the experiences you could need for your space flight. You will learn what it is like to be in zero gravity and others learn the necessary systems.

Weather

For your launch we will make sure there are optimal launch conditions so it goes smooth and you will be in space in no time.

With our safety protocols you will have a safe and fun flight. The flight will go as planned and you will return to earth safely and on time.
