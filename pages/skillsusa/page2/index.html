Boat and Kayak Rentals!

Pier 68 rents center consoles, pontoon, deck boats and kayaks in the middle keys (Islamorada to Marathon)

Call Pier 68 to reserve your center console fishing boat. We offer daily, multi-day, weekly and monthly rental rates. We also have fishing and snorkeling gear.

Contact Us!

Boats equipped with:

GPS, Depth Finder/Fish Finder, VHF Radios, USCG Safety Equipment, Livewells, Dive Flag, Gaff, Coolers, Swim Platforms/Ladders, Outriggers offered on larger boats.

CANCELLATION POLICY

You may change / cancel up to two weeks prior to the start of your rental; in extenuating circumstances, within the two weeks, we will do our best to re-rent the boat for you, if not you are responsible for all rental charges. There is no refund for any cancellation unless there is a named storm, i.e. tropical storm or hurricane. If there is a small craft advisory, it is up to the rental company to determine if there will be any refund.
